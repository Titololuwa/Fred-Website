FRED's Marketplace

For Farmers

Growing Communities, Not Just Crops

FRED provides farmers with the resources, connections, and market access they need to build thriving agricultural businesses. Our platform bridges cultural and economic gaps, allowing farmers to grow culturally significant crops while earning fair compensation.

We offer technical assistance, shared equipment access, and a direct connection to consumers who value their unique products and heritage farming knowledge.

Major Challenge

Farmers struggle with unfair pricing, low profits, and limited access to consumers due to costly middlemen and high retail fees, sometimes up to $6,000 per product, making it hard for small farms to grow and compete.

Our Solution

FRED removes middlemen, enabling farmers to earn fair prices and market their products directly to consumers through a streamlined, farm-to-table platform.

How It Works

Simple onboarding process, training support, and ongoing assistance to help farmers succeed.
