Farm Events & Activities

At Fred's Farm, we believe in building community through shared experiences. Our events bring together farmers, consumers, and food enthusiasts to learn, celebrate, and connect. From hands-on workshops to seasonal festivals, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Seasonal Workshops

Learn sustainable farming practices, seasonal cooking techniques, and traditional preservation methods from our expert farmers and guest instructors.

Community Gatherings

Connect with like-minded individuals at our community potlucks, harvest celebrations, and cultural exchange events that celebrate our diverse farming traditions.

Farm Tours

Experience the farm firsthand with guided tours that showcase our sustainable practices, diverse crops, and the stories of the farmers who grow your food.
